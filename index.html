Bio

I am an Associate Professor in the School of Computer Science and Technology at Huazhong University of Science and Technology. I received my PhD in the School of Electronic and Information Engineering at Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2018. I was also a visiting PhD student in the Computer Science Department at University of Pittsburgh, Pittsburgh, PA, USA. My research focuses on improving performance, reliability of storage systems, etc. My papers have been published in major conferences and journals, including DAC, TPDS, TCAD, JSA, TCE, ICCAD, LCTES, MSST, DATE, NPC, GLSVLSI, etc.

I'm looking for motivated students. If you are interested in doing research with me, please feel free to contact me via email.
